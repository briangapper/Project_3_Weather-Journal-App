Hello dear User,

Welcome to the Weather-Journal-App. Thank you for using this great application, which will not disappoint you.

In here, you will be able to enter your zip-code and optionally describe your feelings today.
Your location's current temperature will be accurately returned thanks to an WeatherData API call.

Finally, the output will be added to the database and displayed below in the 'Most Recent Entry' section along with your optional feeling input.
